# Keyboard Shortcuts

Aegis supports several keyboard shortcuts to speed up your workflow.

## General
- `I`: Go to Inbox.
- `D`: Go to Dashboard.
- `S`: Go to Settings.
- `C`: Open the "Report Issue" modal.
- `Ctrl + K`: Open the "Report Issue" modal (alternative).
- `Esc`: Close open modals or clear selections.

## Inbox
- `J`: Move to next ticket.
- `K`: Move to previous ticket.
- `Enter`: Open selected ticket.
- `R`: Refresh the ticket list.

## Bulk Actions (when tickets are selected)
- `A`: Open assignment menu.
- `S`: Open status change menu.
- `P`: Open priority change menu.
- `Esc`: Clear selection.
